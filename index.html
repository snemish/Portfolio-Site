Big Mobile

A collection of mobile ad units from my time at Big Mobile. The majority of this collection involved the creative to have a high engagement rate while still being impactful and generating awareness for the brands. I was responsible seeing this work through from initial concept stages to the final executions. The final units were developed using HTLM5, CSS3 and Jquery.

Layered

A mobile phone application design that lets you know how many clothing layers you need to be wearing before you head out the door. The application relies on crowdsourcing information through other users in order to provide a more accurate forecast for your area. The app was inspired by little hometown windy Wellington.
